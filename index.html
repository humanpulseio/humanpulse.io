Why we don't work on contingency.

Contingency search creates misaligned incentives. When a recruiter only gets paid if a candidate is placed, speed can win over fit. Our retained model means we're focused entirely on finding the right person for your team, not the fastest one to close.

1
We're a partner, not a broker

Retained engagements align our incentives entirely with yours: finding the right hire, not just any hire.

2
Better outcomes, less pressure

Without commission pressure, we can take the time to get it right and give you our honest assessment.

3
Real partnership

You get a strategic thought partner embedded in the process, not someone checking in only when they have a candidate.
